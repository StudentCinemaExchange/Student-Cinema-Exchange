How the exchange model works

+

Student Cinema Exchange supports classroom teaching and student growth at the same time. Faculty use peer films for instruction and critique, while students watch work from across the country to learn craft, discover trends, and improve their own filmmaking.

+
+
+

1) Upload nominees

+

Departments and festivals nominate standout films with rights documentation and releases.

+
+
+

2) Screen peer work

+

Teachers curate screenings for class inspiration, analysis, and discussion, and students can learn from national peer work.

+
+
+

3) Share revenue

+

Student filmmakers earn 50% of every paid view, with transparent reporting each cycle.
